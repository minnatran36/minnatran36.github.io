Almost Funny

An ongoing collection of things that amuse me.

Power
Power’s weird.
In my family, I fear my son.
He fears my husband.
But my husband fears me.
Being kind
The thing about being kind is—you also need to be specific.
The other day, I decided to give away some stuff we didn’t need anymore. I set it all on a table in front of our house with a sign that said “Free.”
The next morning, everything was still there.
But the table was gone.
Life
Life’s unfair. You can still get athlete’s foot by being a couch potato.
Flattery
When my GPS said, “Go northeast,” I was confused… but also a little flattered.
Like—wow, this intelligent machine really believes I know where north is.
Cute in a quirky way
People say I’m cute “in a quirky way.” Which I think means: technically not cute, but interesting enough that it confuses the eye.
It's like that song—You say it best when you say nothing at all.
Which could mean either:
“There’s something rare and beautiful about you that transcends words.”
Or: “Honestly, you peak when you shut up.”
Compliment or insult? No idea. But I’ll take it.
